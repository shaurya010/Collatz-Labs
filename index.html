About Us

Getting ready for quantum, one verified step at a time.

Lattice is a cryptographic-assurance company. We build tools that let engineering teams prove things about their security posture — to auditors, customers, or regulators — without ever handing over the source code, keys, or business logic behind it.

Our Mission

Secure everything against quantum attacks. The first step is knowing what you're running today — that's zkAssess. The second is planning your move to quantum-safe algorithms, hybrid PQC or full PQC, based on what step one finds.

Step 1 · Live

Analyze what you have

zkAssess scans your current code for quantum-vulnerable and classically weak cryptography, without ever seeing it leave your boundary.

→
Step 2 · Roadmap

Plan the migration

Use those findings to choose and adopt a quantum-safe path — hybrid PQC alongside your existing algorithms, or a full move to PQC.
